Resources

There is a book on how to build arcade machines that I highly recommend: www.projectarcade.com

Most arcade games you'll want can be played with the MAME emulator. Most roms are available at RomNation.

Certain games will require other emulators like DAPHNE . This one is intended in particular for the early 80s laser disk games like Dragon's Lair and Space Ace. You typically can't download the roms for these games, so I bought mine from Digital Leisure.

My controller was made by X-Arcade and my gun was made by Act Labs .

You can buy almost any arcade part from Happ Controls. T-molding is available from www.t-molding.com.
